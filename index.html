How to play

The scavenger hunt is broken up into categories based off the New Jersey State Museum, Pretty Big Things exhibit. First you'll want to select your category, after choosing which one you want to try, you'll start to answer questions on the different pieces in that collection.

The answers to these questions come from both looking at the pieces on display as well as reading the information about it. Also there is a scoring system that allows you to earn a badge, gold, silver and bronze.
